 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 28.5pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">按照山东省教育厅相关要求，为确保我校新生学籍信息准确完整，现启动</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">2023级本科新生</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">学信网学籍信息自查工作，有关要求通知如下。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">一、自查对象</strong></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">202</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">3</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">年录取的普通本科生（含港澳台侨学生）</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">二、自查流程</strong></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">1.</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">新生本人登录“中国高等教育学生信息网”（简称“学信网”，网址：</span><a href="http://www.chsi.com.cn/%EF%BC%89"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 255); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; text-decoration: underline; text-underline: single;">http://www.chsi.com.cn/</span></a><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">）。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">2.</span><span style="background: rgb(255, 255, 255); color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">点击导航栏中第二个项目“学籍查询”进入相应页面。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">如果高考报名时曾在阳光高考平台注册过，请使用当时的用户名和密码，点击“登录学信档案”登录；</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">如果尚未注册，请点击右侧的“注册学信网账号”按钮，按照要求进行实名注册（注意：务必所有信息准确，并填写高考报名时登记的身份证号码，否则可能导致学籍信息无法关联），注册成功后，登录学信档案页面。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">注册后请务必牢记密码，一旦遗忘，找回密码过程十分繁琐，</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">会给将来的</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">学历认定、各类成绩认证、就业考研等带来不便。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">3</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">.</span><span style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">登录成功后，点击上面导航栏中的“高等教育信息”，在下拉条中点击“学籍”，进行就读学校核验，请填写“山东大学”。核验通过后，就会显示出学生本人的学籍信息，请在此页面检查所有个人信息即可。</span></p>
 <p style="text-align: left; line-height: 31.5pt; text-indent: 27.75pt; margin-top: 0pt; margin-right: 0%; margin-bottom: 0pt; page-break-after: auto;"><strong style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); text-transform: none; letter-spacing: 0pt; font-family: 仿宋; font-size: 14pt; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal;">三、自查内容</strong></p>
